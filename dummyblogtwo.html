Introduction to Deep Learning

What is deep learning?

Deep learning is a term often used interchangeably with machine learning. However, they are not the same. Machine learning is a form of artificial intelligence in which computers learn without being told to do something. DL, on the other hand, is the process by which a machine learns to do something using an artificial neural network consisting of many layers organized hierarchically.
